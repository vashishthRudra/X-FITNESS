Bulking Diet Plan

Introduction to Bulking Diet

The bulking diet plan is designed to help increase muscle mass and overall body weight through a calorie surplus and nutrient-rich foods.

Benefits of Bulking Diet

  • Promotes muscle growth
  • Increases strength and power
  • Improves recovery and energy levels
  • Supports overall weight gain
  • Enhances workout performance

Bulking Diet Plans

🍗 Non-Veg Bulking Diet Plan (~2700–3200 kcal/day)

🕖 Meal 1 – Breakfast

  • 4 whole eggs (boiled/scrambled/omelet)
  • 2 slices whole wheat/multigrain bread or 1 cup oats with milk
  • 1 banana or handful of mixed fruits
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter or nuts

🥛 Meal 2 – Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 glass full-fat milk or whey protein shake
  • 8–10 almonds or 5–6 walnuts
  • 1 boiled egg or Greek yogurt

🍗 Meal 3 – Lunch

  • 150–200g grilled chicken breast or lean fish (like tilapia, rohu)
  • 1–1.5 cup cooked white/brown rice or 3 chapatis
  • 1 bowl dal or chickpeas
  • Mixed veggies stir-fried or steamed
  • Salad with olive oil dressing

☕ Meal 4 – Pre-Workout Snack

  • 1 boiled potato or 1 banana + 1 boiled egg
  • Black coffee or pre-workout (optional)

💪 Meal 5 – Post-Workout

  • Whey protein shake (1 scoop with water or milk)
  • 2–3 dates or 1 banana

🍽️ Meal 6 – Dinner

  • 150–200g grilled chicken/fish/boiled eggs
  • 1 medium sweet potato or 2 chapatis
  • Mixed veggies
  • Paneer/tofu for extra protein (if needed)

🌙 Optional – Before Bed

  • 1 glass milk with turmeric or casein protein
  • Cottage cheese or Greek yogurt (slow-digesting protein)

🥦 Vegetarian Bulking Diet Plan (~2700–3200 kcal/day)

🕖 Meal 1 – Breakfast

  • 1 cup oats cooked in full-fat milk
  • Add: 1 tbsp peanut butter + chopped banana + chia/flax seeds
  • 3–4 boiled eggs (or replace with paneer/tofu scramble)
  • 5–6 soaked almonds or walnuts
  • Black coffee or tea

🥛 Meal 2 – Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt or curd
  • 1 banana or apple
  • 1 scoop plant-based or whey protein (if you take dairy)

🍛 Meal 3 – Lunch

  • 1 bowl dal or rajma/chana curry
  • 1.5 cups cooked rice or 3 whole wheat chapatis
  • 1 cup paneer (100–150g) or tofu
  • Steamed/sauteed mixed veggies
  • Salad with olive oil

🍠 Meal 4 – Evening Snack / Pre-Workout

  • 1 sweet potato or boiled corn or peanut chaat
  • 1 glass banana + milk smoothie with oats and honey

💪 Meal 5 – Post-Workout

  • Whey or plant protein shake with water or milk
  • 2–3 dates or 1 banana

🍽️ Meal 6 – Dinner

  • 1 bowl moong dal / chickpeas / soy chunks curry
  • 2–3 chapatis or 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup paneer or tofu bhurji
  • Mixed veggie stir-fry or sabzi

🌙 Optional – Before Bed

  • 1 glass warm milk with turmeric or a scoop of casein protein
  • Cottage cheese / paneer (slow-digesting protein)
