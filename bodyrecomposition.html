Body Recomposition Diet Plan

Introduction to Body Recomposition

Body recomposition focuses on simultaneously losing fat and gaining muscle through a balanced diet and exercise regimen.

Benefits of Body Recomposition

  • Reduces body fat while building muscle
  • Improves metabolic rate
  • Enhances muscle tone and definition
  • Supports sustainable weight management
  • Boosts overall fitness and health

Body Recomposition Diet Plans

🌱 Vegetarian Body Recomp Diet Plan (~2000–2200 kcal/day)

⚖️ Goal: Slight calorie deficit on rest days, slight surplus or maintenance on training days. High protein, moderate carbs and healthy fats.

🕖 Meal 1 – Breakfast

  • 1 cup oats with water or skim milk
  • Add: chia/flax seeds + 1/2 banana + cinnamon
  • 2 boiled eggs or tofu scramble (or 1 scoop plant protein)
  • Green tea or black coffee

🥛 Meal 2 – Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 scoop whey/plant protein shake
  • 5–6 almonds or 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 apple or seasonal fruit

🍛 Meal 3 – Lunch

  • 1 bowl dal or chickpeas/kidney beans (protein!)
  • 1.5 chapatis or 1 cup cooked brown rice/quinoa
  • 100–150g paneer or tofu
  • Steamed/spiced veggies (broccoli, beans, carrots)
  • Cucumber & lemon salad

☕ Meal 4 – Pre-Workout Snack

  • 1 small sweet potato or banana
  • Black coffee
  • Handful of roasted chana

💪 Meal 5 – Post-Workout

  • 1 scoop whey/plant protein in water or milk
  • 2 dates or a banana (simple carbs for recovery)

🌙 Meal 6 – Dinner

  • Stir-fried tofu/paneer with veggies (zucchini, bell pepper, spinach)
  • 1 multigrain chapati or small portion rice
  • Moong dal or light curry

🛌 Optional – Before Bed

  • Low-fat curd or 1/2 cup paneer (slow-digesting protein)
  • Herbal tea (chamomile or peppermint)

🍗 Non-Vegetarian Body Recomposition Diet Plan (~2000–2200 kcal/day)

🎯 On training days: Slight surplus or maintenance
On rest days: Slight deficit
Goal = Preserve muscle + burn fat

🕖 Meal 1 – Breakfast

  • 3 whole eggs (boiled/scrambled) + 2 egg whites
  • 2 slices whole wheat bread or 1 small bowl oats
  • 1/2 banana or apple
  • Green tea or black coffee

🥛 Meal 2 – Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 5–6 almonds or 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 fruit (like papaya, orange, or apple)

🍛 Meal 3 – Lunch

  • 150g grilled chicken breast / lean fish (like tilapia)
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice or 2 chapatis
  • 1 bowl dal or boiled chickpeas
  • Mixed salad (greens, cucumber, tomato, olive oil)
  • Steamed veggies
