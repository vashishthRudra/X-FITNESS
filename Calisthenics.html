Introduction to Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a form of strength training consisting of a variety of movements which exercise large muscle groups, such as push-ups, pull-ups, squats, and dips, using your own body weight.

Benefits of Calisthenics

  • Builds functional strength and flexibility
  • Improves balance and coordination
  • Enhances muscle endurance
  • Can be done anywhere without equipment
  • Suitable for all fitness levels
